LifeExpectancy

Life expectancy is a number that indicates the years a person has left to live, on average. It depends on many factors, ranging to the actual age of the person considered to their social status and nationality. In this project we want to explore the meaning of life expectancy and go beyond the number, to find out the real information this value carries inside. From now on, whenever life expectancy (LE) is mentioned in a chart, unless otherwise mentioned, it's intended to be life expectancy calculated at birth.
