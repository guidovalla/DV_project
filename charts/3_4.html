The importance of basic vaccines coverage for children
In this bubble chart is possible to see the LE of various countries compared to the % of population children vaccinated with a selected vaccine. Each bubble represents a country, with their size depending on the population and colour depedning on their continent, as seen in the legend on the top right. By hovering a bubble a tooltip appears, indicating the name of the country, the exact %of children vaccinated for each selectable disease. On the bottom of the chart a year slider and a vaccine selector are present, by clicking and dragging it it's possible to change the year visualized on the chart and by clicking a different vaccine the chart will visualize data related to the selected vaccine
