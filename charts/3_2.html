Chart 2

In this choropleth chart is possible to see the children mortality in children under 1 year. The colour of each country depends on the value of the children mortality in the country, following the legend in the bottom right of the chart. A tooltip appears when hovering a country, showing the exact value of the parameter in that country. On the bottom of the chart a slider is present: by clicking and dragging the slider is possible to change the year visualized in the chart
