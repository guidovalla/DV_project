Life expectancy vs GDP per capita
In this bubble chart is possible to see the LE of various countries compared to their GDP per capita (Preston curve). The x axis is on a logarithmic scale for a better visualization, in order to avoid clogging the left end of the chart. Each bubble represents a country, with their size depending on the population and colour depending on their continent, as seen in the legend on the top right. By hovering a bubble a tooltip appears, indicating the name of the country, the exact value of GDP per capita and their population. On the bottom of the chart a year slider is present, by clicking and dragging it it's possible to change the year visualized on the chart.
