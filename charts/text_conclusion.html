Life Expectancy tells us a story. A story about a species, faced with many ever changing challenges and hurdles, who keeps adapting to their envirorment to fulfill their desire for a better life. While many inequalities still stand and new problems keep showing up, our growth in recent years and the projections for our future look better than what many would think.
